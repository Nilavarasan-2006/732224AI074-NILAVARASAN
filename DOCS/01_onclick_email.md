- Sign in n8n

- Step 1:
Open your n8n instance URL (e.g., https://app.n8n.cloud or your self-hosted domain).

- Step 2:
Click the “Sign In” button on the top-right.

- Step 3:
Enter your email and password, or choose Google/GitHub to sign in.

- Step 4:
Click “Log In” to access your n8n dashboard.
